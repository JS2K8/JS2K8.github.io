Password Generator

This is a web app for generating complex but memorable passwords. This is accomplished by combining words from a list of adjectives and nouns, a random symbol, and a three digit number as opposed to the usual random combination of letters, numbers, and symbols which are generated by most password generators. This makes it more memorable but doesn't compromise security. A random password generated by this program, tested at howsecureismypassword.net, is likely to take a computer over a quadrillion years to crack.

Dice Game
